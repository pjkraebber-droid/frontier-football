## 📌 FRONTIER NOTE — USL Premier (launching 2028)

The pyramid is being built from the top down. 
USL Premier (Division One) will operate as a single national table, with a long-term target of 20 clubs, and is scheduled to launch in 2028.
 
The USL is currently reviewing applications from clubs interested in competing in USL Premier and will announce the league's inaugural members at a later date.


Critically: 
as USL Premier takes shape alongside the connected structure, the USL will introduce America's first promotion and relegation system — creating a merit-based professional pathway that rewards performance and raises the level of competition across all divisions.
 
No details have been given on how pro/rel would be implemented between leagues, and no teams have been officially announced for USL Premier.


For clubs in USL Championship and League One, the calculus on ownership investment, stadium infrastructure, and roster quality is shifting right now, in real time. Every point won in 2026 is being watched against a backdrop where the table might eventually mean something existential.

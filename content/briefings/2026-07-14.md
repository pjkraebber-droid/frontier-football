### 📌 FRONTIER NOTE — USL Premier (2028) Pipeline Taking Shape

The USL Premier (Tier 1, launching 2028) applicant list is growing. 
Louisville City FC, North Carolina FC, Pittsburgh Riverhounds SC, and Sporting Club Jacksonville have applied to join.
 The stadium requirements are the central constraint. 
An upgrade of F.N.B. Stadium to meet the stadium capacity requirement was put forward as the centerpiece of the Riverhounds' bid.
 
The USL requires a minimum 15,000-seat, purpose-built soccer stadium that will serve as an anchor for real estate development.
 For NCFC, 
the club announced it was suspending operations pending an application to join the future USL Premier in 2028
, while 
despite acquiring the needed land and some regulatory approvals, owner Steve Malik hasn't been able to secure public financing for the stadium, and the men's team averaged just 2,550 fans per game — ranking 21st out of 24 teams in the USL Championship — before going dormant.
 The Riverhounds and Louisville are structurally better positioned; 
Birmingham Legion FC, Miami FC, and Oakland Roots SC currently meet both the market population and capacity requirements, while Louisville City meets the capacity requirement.
